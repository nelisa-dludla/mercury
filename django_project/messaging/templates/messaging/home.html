About Yap

Connecting You Instantly, Effortlessly.

Yap is a real-time messaging app designed to make communication effortless and instant. Whether you're chatting with friends, family, or colleagues, Yap ensures seamless and reliable conversations. Built with simplicity and efficiency in mind, Yap is perfect for those who value quick and easy connectivity. As a portfolio project, Yap showcases my skills in developing a functional and user-friendly application from scratch.

Features

Everything You Need for Seamless Conversations.

Real-Time Messaging

Experience instant communication with friends and family. Messages are delivered and received in real-time, ensuring you stay connected without delay.

User Authentication

Secure sign-up and login processes to keep your conversations private and protected.

Message History

Access your past conversations with ease. Yap stores your chat history, allowing you to revisit important messages anytime.

Typing Indicators

Know when someone is typing a message to you. Yap includes typing indicators to make your conversations more dynamic.

Responsive Design

Enjoy a seamless experience on both desktop and mobile devices. Yap's responsive design ensures the app looks great and works well on any screen size.
